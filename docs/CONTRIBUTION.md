# NEXUS — Research Contribution Statement

This work presents NEXUS, a Kubernetes-native Internal Developer Platform
implementing the closed-loop AIOps paradigm.

References: Dang et al. 2019, Chen et al. 2020, Notaro et al. 2021.

## Three Concrete Contributions

1. Self-service infrastructure provisioning platform — reducing developer
   lead time from days to minutes, empirically measured.

2. Anomaly detection benchmark: Z-score vs Isolation Forest vs LSTM
   autoencoder on Prometheus time-series under controlled fault injection.

3. First open-source integration of RAG-augmented runbook retrieval
   with LLM-based incident reasoning in a Kubernetes operator,
   with measured precision and recall on a defined incident taxonomy.

## What This Is NOT Claiming
- This is not a novel algorithm or theoretical contribution
- This is an engineering thesis: design, implement, measure, compare
- The Cognitive Loop implements known closed-loop AIOps patterns
